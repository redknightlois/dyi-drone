# LM2596 DC-DC Buck Converter Module — Reference Specs

Adjustable step-down converter with built-in 3-digit LED voltmeter display,
two control buttons (ON/OFF and IN/OUT mode), voltage-adjustment potentiometer,
and IN/OUT indicator LEDs.

### Control Buttons (2×, tactile switches)

| Parameter | Left Button | Right Button |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Label | ON/OFF | IN/OUT |
| Size | ~3.5 × 3.5 mm | ~3.5 × 3.5 mm |
| Height above PCB | ~3 mm | ~3 mm |

**Left Button (ON/OFF) Functions:**
- Short press: toggle voltmeter display on/off
- Long press >1 s: shut down voltmeter display
- Long press >4 s: enter self-calibration mode (range ±0.5 V)

**Right Button (IN/OUT) Functions:**
- Short press: switch display between input voltage (IN LED on) and output voltage (OUT LED on)
- Long press >2 s (calibration mode): switch between IN/OUT calibration, save value

## Lid Access Requirements

When designing a lid/cover for the enclosure, these components **require** or
**benefit from** through-holes or windows:

| # | Component | Access Type | Min Opening | Required? |
|---|-----------|------------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | LED Display | Rectangular window | 22 × 13 mm | **Yes** — must see voltage reading |
| 2 | Potentiometer | Circular hole (screwdriver) | ∅4.0 mm | **Yes** — adjust output voltage |
| 3 | ON/OFF Button | Circular hole or slot | ∅3.6 mm | **Yes** — toggle/calibrate display |
| 4 | IN/OUT Button | Circular hole or slot | ∅3.6 mm | **Yes** — switch IN/OUT mode |
| 5 | IN LED | Small window or light pipe | ∅2.0 mm | Recommended — see active mode |
| 6 | OUT LED | Small window or light pipe | ∅2.0 mm | Recommended — see active mode |
| 7 | Heatsink area | Ventilation slots | ~15 × 12 mm zone | Recommended — thermal management |
| 8 | Screw terminals | Wall cutouts (not lid) | 12 × 10 mm per side | **Yes** — wire routing (±X walls) |

> **Note:** Items 1–4 are mandatory for normal operation. Items 5–7 improve
> usability and thermal performance. Item 8 is a wall feature, not a lid feature.
